APCED 2016
Asia-Pacific Conference on Economic Dynamics

Over the last few decades, economists from all disciplines of the field havecome to recognize the value of adopting dynamic rather than static models inorder to capture and accurately represent the essential features of moderneconomic systems. Through improved understanding of dynamic process theory,stability analysis and dynamic optimization, researchers have shed new lighton the evolution of a host of economic phenomena, from macroeconomicfluctuations to growth and development, asset and commodity prices, exchangerates, technological progress, exploitation of natural resources, climate change, consumer behavior and the dynamic interactions of competing firms. Despite the progress made to date, much work on these topics remains to bedone.

Call For Papers


The Asia-Pacific Conference on Economic Dynamics (APCED) 2016 cordially invites you to participate in the Conference on  1st – 2nd December 2016 in Ho Chi Minh City, Vietnam.

Topics Original research papers in all areas of economic dynamics are welcome.
Participants

Academics, researchers, and practitioners are encouraged to apply.

University students and recent graduates are particularly encouraged to respond to the call for papers.
Submission

All papers must be submitted electronically before 15th October 2016 via email address (dmhoang@hcmiu.edu.vn).

Presentations of the selected papers, research projects at the conference must be in English.
Paper review

Submitted papers will be acknowledged by the organizers via email and will be reviewed by the Program Committee.

Newspaper and television reporters are welcome to register for an official seat at the two-day conference (see Registration for details).
